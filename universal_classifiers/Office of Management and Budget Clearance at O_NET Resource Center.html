O*NET Data Collection

On November 14, 2024, OMB approved external site a three-year extension of the O*NET Data Collection effort. In 2023, the O*NET project sought OMB approval for this extension. As part of the clearance process, public comments were sought on the data collection. A Federal Register Notice external site, published on July 31, 2023, announced the availability to the public of the O*NET "Information Collection Request" (ICR). A second Federal Register Notice external site, published on January 17, 2024, announced an additional comment period which concluded on February 16, 2024.

Data Publication Schedule

The O*NET 30.3 database represents the most recent update resulting from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approved data collection program. This database release includes an update of 78 occupations from job incumbent and occupational expert sources. The schedule below provides both past and targeted publication dates, along with the numbers of occupations scheduled for update during each release period.

895 occupations have been updated by job incumbents and occupational experts to date. You can view the full list of updated occupations online. 776 of these occupations have more than one update. You can view the list of occupations with more than one update as well.

Occupational Code Assignment

On February 12, 2026, OMB approved external site a three-year extension of the Occupational Code Assignment (OCA) process. In 2025, the Department of Labor sought OMB approval for this extension. A Federal Register Notice external site, published on October 24, 2024, announced the availability to the public of the "Information Collection Request" (ICR). A second Federal Register Notice external site, published on April 14, 2025, announced the submission for OMB review.
